Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

Introduction

Blossom ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

We believe your journey to quit smoking should be personal and private. That's why we've built Blossom with privacy at its core.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and name (if you create an account)
  • Profile Data: Quit date, smoking habits (cigarettes per day, pack price), and personal motivation
  • Journal Entries: Personal notes you choose to save
  • Craving Logs: Information about cravings you track

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, and time spent in the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Track and display your smoke-free progress
  • Calculate savings and health milestones
  • Sync your data across devices (if you create an account)
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Send optional motivational notifications (with your permission)

Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: Your data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Backup: If you create an account, data is securely stored on Supabase servers with encryption
  • Encryption: All data transmitted to our servers uses industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access Control: Only you can access your personal data through your account

We do NOT: Sell your personal information, share your data with advertisers, or use your data for targeted advertising. Your quit journey is yours alone.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Export: Download your data at any time
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications or use the app without an account

Data Retention

  • With Account: Data is retained until you delete your account
  • Without Account: Data is stored locally and deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure authentication and data storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo: For app distribution and updates (Privacy Policy)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of Blossom after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Children's Privacy

Blossom is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
